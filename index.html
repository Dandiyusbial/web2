Why Choose Our Product

ARRA is made of all-natural materials: coconut shell charcoal, and a small portion of natural starch as binder. Our shisha natural coal is odourless and long glow times. ARRA combines excellent taste properties with a first-rate quality and a real sustainability. Our premium coconut shell briquettes are suitable for shisha and grilling BBQ.
